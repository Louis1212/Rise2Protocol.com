Stacking plateform

Staking: you deposit tokens and receive income until you withdraw what you have deposited. On most platforms, you will be able to deposit either the platform's governing crypto-currency (e.g. RISE2PROTOCOL on our platform), or a high-cap crypto-currency (e.g. BNB). By staking your cryptos, you are simply guaranteeing the platform that you will not sell your tokens, and therefore the platform will reward you for as long as you keep them. Staking also has the effect of increasing the "TVL" on the platform. This value corresponds to the amount in dollars of everything that is deposited on the site, whether in farming or in staking. An increase in the TVL usually increases the price of the platform's governance token, since it often attracts new users to the platform.
